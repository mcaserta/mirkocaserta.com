[Noises](https://www.audiothing.net/instruments/noises/) is a beautifully
crafted noise generator instrument by [AudioThing](https://www.audiothing.net/).
It captures the textures and character of real-world noise sources — tape hiss,
vinyl crackle, old machinery, rain, and much more — and turns them into playable,
expressive instruments.

![Noises screenshot](/images/posts/noises-scrot.jpg)

I've been spending some time with it and made a bunch of presets that I'm pretty
happy with. They cover a range of moods and textures: some are ambient and
meditative, others are gritty and industrial, and a few are just plain weird.

You can download them here: [NoisesUserPresets.zip](/music/NoisesUserPresets.zip)

To install, unzip the archive and drop the preset files into your Noises user
presets folder. On macOS that's typically:

```
~/Library/Application Support/AudioThing/Noises/Presets/User
```

Enjoy the noise.
